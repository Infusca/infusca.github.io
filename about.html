Hi, my name is Nichola Holcomb.

I am a pharmacist. I work in retail, at Walgreens. I have worked for Walgreens since about 2018 or so, when they bought out Rite Aid. I was with Rite Aid originally since 2005 when I was an intern in college.

Right now in my free time I mostly enjoy programming (websites, like this one, or simple games) & gaming. I also am interested in photography, reading, mountain biking, wood turning, guitar, piano, metal detecting, cooking, traveling, and camping.

I enjoy collecting rocks, fossils, artifacts, and of course coins and relics I find metal detecting. I love all animals, but especially wolves, cats, and dinosaurs.

The name of the website, Smoke on Horizon comes from a lyric from one of my favorite songs from one of my favorite bands: Fire Woman by The Cult.

Feel free to contact me if you are interested in hot sauce, wood turned pens, or other wood turned items.

My Partner, Stef, aka StefDigs

I live with my partner Stef, since 2014, in my house, which is a beautiful log cabin home, in the woods.

When I met Stef, she was an insurance agent. She has since left that to follow a dream in metal detecting. She has worked hard via social media to grow a following and now is affiliated with Minelab (one of the largest metal detecting companies). She is a Minelab Detexpert and also works for The Diggers Den, a dealer, to sell metal detectors and equipment. She has a youtube channel and also has written several articles for Digger Magazine and has had some of her finds shown in the magazine as well as on the cover. She also is a talented musician, albeit out of practice, and can play bass, guitar, and drums.

Be sure to contact Stef if you are ever looking for anything metal detecting related. You can reach her via the social media links below.
